Why join Model UN?

Through training sessions and conferences, delegates are able to develop and strengthen valuable skills that they can carry through the rest of their lives.



Public Speaking

Become comfortable and confident speaking in front of large groups of people, and learn to speak in a professional manner.



Networking

Engage with delegates from other universites all around the world, and become part of the amazing UoG Model UN community.



Education

Become educated on political, social, economical and environmental issues, international relations, and current events across the world, and become exposed to the different perspectives and solutions to these issues.



Boost your resume

Model UN is a great academic, extra-curricular activity to add to your resume. The communication, teamwork and leadership learned through Model UN is an asset to all careers.



Fun!

Between making new friends within UoG and across the world, and the social events (bar nights, semi-formals), Model UN is a ton of fun!
