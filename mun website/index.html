Welcome to The University of Guelph Model United Nations

UoG Model UN is an academic club focused on: education and critical thinking about politics and world issues, as well as encouraging the development of strong teamwork and communication skills.
As a club, we organize trips to Model UN conferences hosted by other Universities, and offer training sessions to promote debate skills and knowledge of procedure in order to prepare delegates for these conferences.

Be sure to follow us on Facebook and Instagram for the latest updates about meetings and conferences.
