Upcoming Events

UoG Model UN participates in several external conferences hosted by other Universities throughout the year, where delegates can engage in debate, solve world issues, and network. For the first time, we will be hosting our own Internal Conference for UoG Delegates in September 2018.

UoG MUN Internal Conference

Our first annual internal conference will be held the weekend of September 29-30th 2018.

This conference is open to all University of Guelph delegates, and will allow every delegate to practise and experience both a General Assembly and Crisis committee. You can also look forward to a social event, food, placards, name tags, awards and an unforgettable experience!

Attending the Internal Conference is MANDATORY for ALL delegates who wish to participate in external conferences.

Registration now open until Tuesday Sept. 18th! e-transfer us (mun@uoguelph.ca) the $20 registration fee!

Please contact us (mun@uoguelph.ca) for more information. Visit our Facebook Event Page to RSVP!

QMUNi: November 15th - 18th 2018

QMUNi is an external conference hosted by Queen's University in Kingston, ON.

QMUNi offers small committees, fun social events and a tight knit community feel that is perfect for first time delegates.

Registration is open until Oct 12th. Contact us for more information on registration.

McMUN is hosted by McGill University in Montreal, ON.
